Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from our MMU community

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also contact us directly with your order number.

Do you deliver to MMU campus?

Yes! We specialize in delivering to Multi Media University of Kenya (MMU). Your orders are our priority and we ensure reliable, on-time delivery to campus.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa (paybill/buy goods) and Cash on Delivery for MMU students. Secure online payments are processed instantly.

How do I return an item?

Contact us within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused with tags attached. We'll guide you through our simple return process.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, we're located in Rongai, Kajiado County. Visit us Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm. MMU students get special in-store discounts!

How can I get style advice?

DM us on Instagram @hokasparlour or email hokasparlour@gmail.com. Our fashion consultants are ready to help you find your look.

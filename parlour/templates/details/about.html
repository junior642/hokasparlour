Our Story

Hoka's Parlour was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bring contemporary, trend-focused fashion to the students of Multi Media University of Kenya (MMU). Founded by Getrine Hoka and co-founding support from Vincent Omwenga, our brand emerged from the vibrant campus life of MMU.

Starting as a small initiative within the MMU community, we recognized that students at Multi Media University of Kenya (MMU) deserved access to quality, stylish clothing that reflects their dynamic personalities and modern campus lifestyle. Our journey began in Rongai, Kajiado County, just a stone's throw away from Nairobi National Park, and quickly grew through word-of-mouth among MMU students.

Today, Hoka's Parlour stands as the premier MMU clothing store, trusted by hundreds of students for our commitment to quality, style, and most importantly, reliable delivery. Every order placed by an MMU student is handled with the utmost care and urgency because we understand that campus life waits for no one.

Did You Know? Hoka's Parlour was conceptualized in the halls of MMU and remains deeply connected to the student community that built it.

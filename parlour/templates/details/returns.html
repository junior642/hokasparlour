Refund & Return Policy

Fairness · Transparency · Trust

At Hoka's Parlour, we are committed to fairness, transparency, and protecting our customers' interests. We understand that sometimes circumstances change or issues may arise with an order. This policy clearly explains how cancellations, refunds, and returns are handled.

1. Order Cancellation Policy
1.1 Cancellation Before Delivery
  • Cancel within Saturday–Thursday before dispatch
  • Friday is delivery day – no cancellations once prepared
Submit via: Official email or website return form

After approval:

  • Confirmation email sent
  • Refund within 3 working days
Working days: Saturday–Thursday (Friday excluded)
2. Out-of-Stock / Store Issues

In rare cases of:

  • Product unavailable
  • Stock miscalculation
  • Logistical issues
Hoka's Parlour will:
  • Notify immediately
  • Cancel order
  • Send confirmation email
  • Full refund within 3 working days
3. Refund Processing Timeline

Duration

Within 3 working days after confirmation email

Method

Original payment method (unless agreed otherwise)

Working Days

Saturday–Thursday Friday excluded

4. Returns After Delivery
  • Complaints within 3 days after delivery
  • After 3 days → null and void
  • Submit via email or website form
Strict 3-day window – inspect your order promptly
5. Return Procedure
Review
Team evaluates case
May request
Photos, order details, explanation
Decision
Approval, exchange, refund, alternative

Final decision communicated via email

6. Conditions for Return Approval

Approved if:

Unused, original condition Within 3 days Matches issue described

Rejected if:

Damaged after delivery Past 3 days Misuse signs

7. Our Commitment

At Hoka's Parlour, we value trust, especially within our MMU community and customers in Rongai. We stand by our responsibility to:

  • Deliver what is ordered
  • Refund fairly when necessary
  • Communicate clearly and professionally

Customer satisfaction remains one of our core values.

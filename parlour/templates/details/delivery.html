Delivery Policy

Hoka's Parlour – Rongai & MMU Community

At Hoka's Parlour, we operate a structured and reliable delivery system to ensure that all customers — especially our MMU and Rongai community — clearly understand when to expect their orders. We currently classify products into two categories: Ready Stock and Warehouse Stock.

1. Ready Stock Next-Day Delivery

Available at our Rongai store – physically in stock.

Delivery Timeline: Orders placed today → Delivered the next working day.

Handled directly by Hoka's Parlour delivery team.

Important Requirements:
  • Provide your Product Number / Order Number during delivery
  • Required to confirm and release your item
  • Failure may delay confirmation
✅ Ensures accuracy, prevents errors
2. Warehouse Stock Friday Delivery

Main Store inventory – delivered weekly.

Delivery Rule

All Warehouse Stock orders delivered on Friday.

Weekly System

  • Order Sat–Thu → Delivered same week Friday
  • Order Friday → Delivered next week Friday

Examples

  • Tuesday order → Friday same week
  • Sunday order → Friday same week
  • Friday order → Next Friday
  • Saturday order → Coming Friday

3. Why Friday?

Warehouse Stock must be transported from Main Store to Rongai. Consolidating deliveries on Friday ensures efficiency and reduces delays.

  • Weekend orders grouped into Friday
